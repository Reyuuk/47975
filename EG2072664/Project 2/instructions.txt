This is the game of battleship. To begin, each player will secretly 
arrange ships on a 10x10 grid. Each ship occupies a number of 
consecutive spaces. Below is a list of the ships and their size.

Aircraft Carrier : 5
Battleship : 4
Sumbarine : 3
Destroyer : 3
Patrol Boat : 2

After boats have been placed by both players, you will take turns attacking
each other's grid until all of your opponent's ships have been sunk!
You can play versus another player or versus a computer controlled AI.

Type 1 to play single player vs AI.
Type 2 to player two player vs another person.
